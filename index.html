Intestment.com will allow you to test programmatic trading algorithms on a number of exchanges and across many different currencies without having to risk your capital. With reverse engineered API's, you can create your trading program to execute against our servers and when your theories are proven, a few simple changes will allow you to execute them against real exchanges and produce real profits.

We constantly strive to add more functionality and expand our platforms to match the most popular markets. If you want new features or markets to be implement, just ask! The robustnest of our exchange allows for new currencies and markets to be added very quickly and allows for rapidly emerging markets to be tested.

FAQ

Do I have to give you my personal information?
Absolutely not! Just create a free account and start executing against the API.
Do I have to access the API programmatically?
No! While the idea of Intestment was born out of programmatic investing, we have a full user interface that will allow trades to be made by hand.
